

## Josua Simatupang — Personal Portfolio Website

### Design Direction: "Minimal & Clean"
A clean, monochrome aesthetic with generous whitespace, one subtle accent color (e.g., a muted blue or teal), and refined typography. Not a clone of Jacky or Clarence — instead, a confident, understated identity that lets your work speak.

### Pages & Sections (Single-page with smooth scroll + separate routes)

**1. Hero Section**
- Bold name: "Josua Simatupang"
- Tagline: "Frontend Engineer" with a short, personal intro sentence about what you build and care about
- Social links (GitHub, LinkedIn, Email) as minimal icons
- Subtle animated entrance (fade-in)

**2. About Summary**
- Brief 2-3 sentence bio highlighting your journey (Civil Engineering → Frontend), your current role at Synergy, and what drives you
- Clean layout with a soft accent divider

**3. Experience Timeline**
- Vertical timeline showing Synergy Financial Advisers and Jakarta Eye Center
- Each entry with role, company, date range, and 2-3 key accomplishments
- Clean cards with hover states

**4. Projects Showcase**
- Grid of project cards for key work (Synergy website, HopOn recruitment platform, AI Chatbot, JEC Dashboard)
- Each card with title, brief description, and tech tags (Next.js, React, TypeScript, etc.)

**5. Tech Stack / Skills**
- Minimal icon/tag cloud showing your technologies: React, Next.js, Vue, TypeScript, Node.js, Tailwind, etc.

**6. Contact Section**
- Clean footer-style contact area with email, LinkedIn, GitHub
- Simple "Let's connect" message
- Optional contact form

### Design Details
- **Color**: White background, dark text, one muted accent color (teal/blue)
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif (Inter or similar), bold headings, light body
- **Spacing**: Generous padding, breathing room between sections
- **Interactions**: Smooth scroll, subtle hover effects, fade-in animations on scroll
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first, looks great on all devices
- **Dark mode toggle**: Optional light/dark switch
